Request Process

How document requests are handled.

1

Submit Request

Choose document type and provide student details.

2

Verify Identity

Registrar verifies student record and request purpose.

3

Prepare Document

Office prepares the official certificate or transcript.

4

Approval

Document is checked and approved according to procedure.

5

Collection

Student receives document or delivery instructions.

FAQ

Registrar questions.

Submit a request with your student ID, faculty and required language. Registrar will verify your academic record.

Can someone collect my document for me?

Collection rules depend on university policy and may require authorization.

How long does document processing take?

Processing time depends on document type, verification and office workload.
