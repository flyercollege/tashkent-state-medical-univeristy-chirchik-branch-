Message from the Director

“We educate doctors with knowledge, skill and humanity.”

Welcome to Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch. Our goal is to create a modern academic environment where students gain strong medical knowledge, practical clinical skills, research thinking and professional ethics.

We are committed to developing a university branch that serves Uzbekistan and the global healthcare community through quality education, innovation, international cooperation and compassionate service.

  • Student-centered medical education
  • Modern laboratories and simulation-based training
  • Clinical partnerships and practical hospital experience
  • Research, innovation and international collaboration
