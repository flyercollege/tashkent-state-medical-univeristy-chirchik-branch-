What To Do

Emergency response steps.

1

Stay Safe

Move away from danger and seek immediate help.

2

Call Emergency

Use local emergency services for urgent danger or medical issues.

3

Notify University

Inform hostel duty, International Office, Student Affairs or call center.

4

Document

Record essential details, location and contact information.

5

Follow Up

Follow university and official guidance until the issue is resolved.

FAQ

Safety questions.

Who should I call in a real emergency?

Call official local emergency services first, then notify the university contact or relevant office.

Who helps international students after arrival?

The International Department, Hostel Office and Student Affairs help international students after arrival.

Can parents contact the university?

Parents can contact the relevant office through the Contact page for non-emergency support.
