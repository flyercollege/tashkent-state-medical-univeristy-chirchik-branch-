Service Routing

Where should students and applicants go?

1

Admissions

For application, documents and admission letters contact Admissions Office or Call Center.

2

International

For visa, invitation letter and arrival support contact International Office.

3

Academic

For schedules, learning resources and records contact dean office or Registrar.

4

Student Life

For clubs, events and welfare contact Student Affairs Center.

5

Research

For scientific clubs and publications contact Research & Innovation Center.

Student & Faculty Support

Centers help the university work as one system.

Centers connect academic departments, administration, students, applicants and partners through organized service pathways.

For Applicants

Admissions, call center and international office guide applicants through first contact to enrollment.

For Students

For Faculty

FAQ

Center questions.

Which center helps international students?

The International Office helps with invitation letters, visas, arrival and adaptation.

Where can I ask general questions?

The Call Center or Contact page can route your question to the correct office.

Where can students find research support?

The Research & Innovation Center and academic departments support research clubs and projects.

Who manages university news?

The Press Service manages official news, announcements and event communication.

Need help finding the right center?

Contact the university and we will route your request.

Use the contact page for admissions, international, registrar, hostel, research, student affairs, press service or administration inquiries.
