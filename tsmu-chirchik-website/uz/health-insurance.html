Medical Support

Where to go for help.

Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch students can use official university routing for health concerns, emergencies and wellbeing questions.

  • Emergency: use local emergency services first
  • Hostel issues: contact hostel duty support
  • International students: contact International Department
  • Academic stress: contact Student Affairs or faculty advisor
  • Medical documentation: keep copies of insurance and ID
SituationRecommended action
Emergency medical issueContact local emergency services, then notify university support.
Non-urgent health concernContact Student Affairs or appropriate medical support channel.
Insurance questionContact International Department or Admissions Office.
Hostel health concernNotify hostel duty staff or Hostel Office.
Mental wellbeing concernContact Student Affairs or Psychology support routing.

FAQ

Health insurance questions.

Is health insurance required?

Students should confirm current insurance requirements with the official university offices before arrival or registration.

Who helps international students with insurance?

The International Department and Admissions Office can guide international students on required documents.

What should I do in an emergency?

Use local emergency services first, then notify the relevant university support office.

Does this page replace official medical advice?

No. This page provides general guidance and routing. Medical decisions should be made by qualified healthcare professionals.
