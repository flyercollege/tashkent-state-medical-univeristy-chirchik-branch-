Privacy Policy of Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch

This Privacy Policy is prepared for the website of Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch. It describes how information may be collected through website forms, online application pages, contact requests, hostel requests, registrar forms, career applications, alumni registration and portal demo pages.

Production note: This page should be reviewed by the official legal and administration teams before launch. Replace placeholder text with the approved institutional privacy policy.

Information We May Collect

Depending on the service, Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch may request information such as name, email, phone number, nationality, faculty choice, academic background, application documents, student ID, document request type, hostel request details or inquiry message.

How Information May Be Used

  • To process admissions and applicant inquiries
  • To provide international student support, invitation guidance and arrival coordination
  • To manage hostel, registrar, finance, career and alumni requests
  • To respond to contact form messages and route questions to the correct office
  • To improve website services and user experience

Student and Applicant Documents

Documents such as passports, academic certificates, transcripts and photos should be handled only through secure official systems. The current website forms are front-end prototypes and should be connected to secure backend storage before production.

Portal and Authentication Data

Student Portal and Faculty Portal pages are demo interfaces. Real portal authentication must use secure institutional login, encrypted transmission and authorized access control.

Data Sharing

Information may be shared internally between authorized offices such as Admissions, Registrar, International Department, Hostel Office, Finance Department, Student Affairs or Administration for official university purposes.

Data Security

Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch should use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, documents and academic records from unauthorized access, loss or misuse.

Contact

For privacy or data protection questions, users may contact the university through the official Contact page.

Privacy Questions

Contact Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch.

If you have questions about website forms, application documents, student data or portal access, contact the relevant university office.
