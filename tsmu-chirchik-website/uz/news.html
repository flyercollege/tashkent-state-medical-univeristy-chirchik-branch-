Ambassador of Egypt to Uzbekistan visits TSMU Chirchik Branch

Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch hosted a distinguished visit from the Embassy of the Arab Republic of Egypt in Uzbekistan. The meeting brought together university leadership, representatives of the Egyptian side, faculty members and students to discuss opportunities for international academic cooperation.

The visit highlighted the importance of strengthening educational relations, supporting international students, expanding cultural understanding and developing future collaboration in medical education, research and student mobility.

During the meeting, participants discussed academic partnerships, medical training, international student support and the role of global cooperation in preparing future healthcare professionals.
