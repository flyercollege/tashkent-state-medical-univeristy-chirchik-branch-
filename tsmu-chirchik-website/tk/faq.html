Admissions

Admissions questions

How can I apply to Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch?

You can apply through the online application portal, choose your faculty and submit your initial applicant details.

Which faculties are available?

General Medicine, Pharmacy, Medical Pediatrics and Psychology are available at Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch.

Can I contact Admissions before applying?

Yes. You can contact the Admissions Office through the Contact page for guidance.

Documents

Document questions

What documents are usually required?

Passport or ID, academic certificate, transcript, photo, application form and translations if required.

Where do I request transcripts?

Current students should contact the Registrar Office for transcripts and official certificates.

Can I upload documents online?

The application page includes demo upload fields. Production upload requires secure backend integration.

Fees & Scholarships

Fees and scholarship questions

Where can I confirm tuition fees?

Official tuition and contract information must be confirmed by the Admissions Office and Finance Department.

Are scholarships guaranteed?

No. Scholarships depend on official policy, eligibility, partner programs and written approval.

Are hostel fees separate?

Hostel fees are generally handled separately and must be confirmed by Hostel Office and Finance Department.

International Students

International student questions

Does Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch accept international students?

Yes. International students can apply and receive guidance through the International Department.

Which countries do students come from?

The university welcomes students from Asia, Africa, the Middle East, CIS countries and other regions.

Is orientation available?

Yes. Orientation helps new students understand academic rules, campus services, hostel guidance and student life.

Visa & Arrival

Visa and arrival questions

When do I receive an invitation letter?

Invitation letter guidance begins after admission confirmation and document verification.

Is airport pickup available?

Arrival support and airport pickup coordination may be arranged for confirmed international students.

Who helps with visa questions?

The International Department supports visa guidance and arrival coordination.

Hostel

Hostel questions

Is hostel accommodation guaranteed?

Hostel placement depends on availability, eligibility and university policy. Apply early for guidance.

Can international students request hostel support?

Yes. International students can request accommodation support after admission confirmation.

Where do I apply for hostel?

Use the Hostel page or contact Hostel Office through the Contact page.

Clinical Practice

Clinical practice questions

Do students get clinical practice?

Yes. Students complete supervised practice through partner hospitals and clinics according to faculty and year.

Which hospitals are partners?

The Clinical Practice page lists regional and Chirchik city hospitals, centers and clinics used for supervised practice.

Is simulation training included?

Yes. The Simulation Center supports clinical skills, OSCE, emergency scenarios and communication training.

Student Services

Student service questions

Is there a student portal?

Yes. A Student Portal demo page is available and can be connected to a secure backend in production.

Are student clubs available?

Yes. Student Life includes medical, research, sports, culture, volunteer, innovation and leadership clubs.

Who do I contact for support?

Use the Contact page to route inquiries to Admissions, Registrar, Hostel, International Office, Student Affairs or Administration.

Still Have Questions?

Contact Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch.

Our offices can route your question to Admissions, International Department, Registrar, Hostel Office, Finance, Clinical Practice, Research or Student Affairs.
