🏥

👥

Student Feedback

1

2

Teach

Deliver curriculum through lectures, labs, seminars and practice.

3

Measure

4

Review

5

Improve

Update curriculum, teaching, support services and resources.

FAQ

Quality questions.

What does quality assurance mean?

It means monitoring and improving teaching, curriculum, assessment, clinical education and student support.

Are student opinions considered?

Yes. Student feedback is an important part of continuous improvement.

Where are accreditations shown?

Official accreditation and recognition information should be published only after administrative confirmation.

Who manages quality improvement?

Quality assurance works with administration, academic affairs, faculties, departments and student services.
