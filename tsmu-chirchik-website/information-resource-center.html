FAQ

Information Resource Center questions.

Who can use the Information Resource Center?

Students, faculty and staff of Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch can use academic resources according to university rules.

Are digital resources available?

The page is prepared for digital library and e-resource integration. Official access rules should be confirmed by the center.

Can I get help with research sources?

Yes. Students can request guidance for literature search, citations and research writing.

Can international students use the library?

Yes. International students can receive orientation and academic resource support.
