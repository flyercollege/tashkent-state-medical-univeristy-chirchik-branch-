Eligibility

Who can apply?

Applicants should have completed secondary education or an equivalent qualification and be academically prepared for university-level medical or health sciences education.

  • Completed secondary school or equivalent
  • Science background recommended for medical faculties
  • Valid passport or national ID
  • Readiness to study in the selected language of instruction
  • Medical fitness documents if required
Required Documents

Application checklist.

  • Passport copy / national ID
  • Academic certificate and transcript
  • Passport-size photo
  • Application form
  • Medical certificate where required
  • Translations / notarization if required
  • Parent/guardian consent for minors if applicable

FAQ

Admissions questions.

Common questions about recognition, documents, fees, hostel, international applicants and visa support.

Can international students apply?

Yes. International applicants can apply online and receive document, invitation letter and visa guidance after admission confirmation.

Is hostel accommodation available?

Hostel guidance is available subject to capacity and university policy.

Where can I confirm tuition fees?

Official tuition fees must be confirmed by the Admissions Office and Finance Department.

Which faculties are available?

General Medicine, Pharmacy, Medical Pediatrics and Psychology are available.

Do students get clinical practice?

Yes. Clinical practice is supported through approved partner hospitals and clinics according to faculty and academic year.
