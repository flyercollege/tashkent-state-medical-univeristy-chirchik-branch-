Why this matters

Clinical practice is one of the strongest parts of TSMU Chirchik Branch.

Students do not learn medicine only from books. Through our partner hospital network, students observe real patient care, join supervised practical training, understand hospital workflow, develop clinical reasoning, and connect classroom knowledge with healthcare practice.

The network combines regional multidisciplinary centers, neonatology, infectious diseases, psychiatry and narcology, children’s care, emergency medicine, family outpatient centers and private clinics. This gives students a broad and realistic view of modern healthcare.
