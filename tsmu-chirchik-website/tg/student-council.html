FAQ

Student Council questions.

Can first-year students join clubs?

Yes. First-year students are encouraged to join clubs and participate in campus activities.

Who manages student clubs?

Student Council and Student Affairs coordinate club activities with faculty and administration support.

Can international students join clubs?

Yes. International students can join clubs and participate in cultural, academic and volunteer activities.
