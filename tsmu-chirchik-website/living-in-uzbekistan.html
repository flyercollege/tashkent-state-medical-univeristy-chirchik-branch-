Student Guide

What international students should know.

Living in Uzbekistan is a new experience for many students. Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch supports students through orientation, international office guidance and student services.

🏙️

Chirchik City

A student-friendly city in Tashkent Region with access to campus, clinics, transport and daily services.

🚆

Transport

Students can access local transport and travel routes to Tashkent with guidance from senior students and staff.

🏠

Accommodation

Cost of Living

Plan your student budget carefully.

Costs depend on personal lifestyle, accommodation, food, transport and academic needs. The university can provide general guidance, but students should plan responsibly.

  • Accommodation or hostel fee
  • Food and daily personal expenses
  • Local transport and communication
  • Study materials and stationery
  • Health insurance or medical support where required
  • Emergency reserve for unexpected needs

FAQ

Living in Uzbekistan questions.

Is Chirchik close to Tashkent?

Chirchik is located in Tashkent Region and has access to the wider Tashkent area.

Can international students get hostel support?

Yes. International students can request hostel support after admission confirmation, subject to availability.

Is orientation provided?

Yes. Orientation introduces academic rules, campus services, hostel guidance and student support.

Who should I contact after arrival?

Contact the International Department, Admissions Office, Hostel Office or Student Affairs depending on the question.
