Publication Ethics

Responsible scientific writing at Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch.

Every publication should respect academic honesty, proper citation, confidentiality and research integrity.

Quality Assurance

Originality

Avoid plagiarism and submit honest academic work.

Citation

Reference all sources clearly and correctly.

Confidentiality

Protect patient and participant information.
