Rules & Responsibilities

A respectful, safe and study-focused community.

Hostel life requires discipline, respect and shared responsibility. Students are expected to follow university rules, respect neighbors and maintain a clean environment.

  • Respect quiet hours and study time
  • Follow security and visitor procedures
  • Keep rooms and shared spaces clean
  • Report maintenance issues early
  • Respect cultural diversity and student privacy
  • Follow hostel administration instructions

FAQ

Hostel questions.

Common questions about rooms, fees, safety, dining and international student support.

Is hostel accommodation guaranteed?

Hostel placement depends on availability, student eligibility and university policy. Students should apply early.

Can international students request hostel placement?

Yes. International students can request accommodation support after admission confirmation.

Are hostel fees included in tuition?

Official fee details must be confirmed by Hostel Office and Finance Department.

Is the hostel safe?

Student safety is supported through rules, duty contacts, security procedures and hostel administration.

Who do I contact for hostel questions?

Contact Hostel Office through the Contact page or submit the hostel request form.
