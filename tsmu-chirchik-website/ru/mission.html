Mission, Vision and Strategic Goals

Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch is committed to educating skilled, ethical and compassionate healthcare professionals through academic excellence, clinical practice, research, international cooperation and community service.

Vision

To become a leading medical education branch in Central Asia.

Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch aims to be recognized for academic excellence, clinical training, scientific research, international cooperation and healthcare leadership.

  • Regional leadership in medical education
  • Internationally connected academic environment
  • Modern clinical and simulation-based learning
  • Research culture and healthcare innovation
Mission

To educate skilled, ethical and compassionate healthcare professionals.

The mission of Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch is to provide high-quality education, practical training, research opportunities and community engagement that prepare students to serve society responsibly.

  • Quality education and modern curriculum
  • Clinical practice and patient-centered learning
  • Scientific thinking and research participation
  • Service to communities in Uzbekistan and beyond
