Academic Model

How students learn at Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch.

The academic model combines medical knowledge, practice, research, ethics and professional communication.

📚

Modern Curriculum

Programs are organized around learning outcomes, discipline modules and practical competencies.

🧩

Problem-Based Learning

Clinical cases help students connect theory with patient-centered problem solving.

🧪

Practical Education

Laboratories, simulation rooms, seminars and supervised practice support active learning.

🏥

Clinical Rotations

Partner hospitals help students understand real healthcare systems and patient care.

💻

Technology Integration

Digital resources, portals and e-learning tools support independent study.

🔬

Research

Student scientific clubs, presentations and research projects build academic confidence.

⚖️

Professionalism, confidentiality, patient dignity and academic honesty guide learning.

FAQ

Academic questions.

What academic system is used?

Tashkent State Medical University Chirchik Branch uses structured curriculum, credit-based learning and competency-focused assessment.

Yes. Clinical practice is connected with partner hospitals and faculty-specific practical training.

Schedules and academic notices can be provided through faculties, educational sections and the student portal.
