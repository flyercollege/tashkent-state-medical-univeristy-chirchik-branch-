FAQ

Alumni questions.

Who can register as alumni?

Graduates of the university branch or official alumni network members can register.

Can alumni mentor students?

Yes. Alumni can volunteer for career talks, mentorship days and student guidance.

Can I submit my success story?

Yes. Use the registration form or contact the Alumni Office to share your story.

Are alumni events held regularly?

Alumni events can include reunions, seminars, mentoring sessions and professional networking.
