Press Workflow

How stories become official news.

1

Submit

Faculty, office or student group submits news or event details.

2

3

4

5

Publish

News is published on official website and channels.

FAQ

Press Service questions.

Who can submit news?

Departments, faculties, offices, student organizations and authorized representatives can submit news for review.

Are all submissions published?

No. Submissions must be verified, edited and approved according to university communication standards.

Can students submit achievements?

Yes. Student achievements can be submitted through faculty, student affairs or Press Service channels.
