FAQ

Campus tour questions.

Can parents visit the campus?

Yes. Parents and applicants can request a guided visit through Admissions Office.

Is a virtual 360° tour available?

This page is prepared for 360° media. Real panoramic images can be added in production.

Can international applicants request a visit?

Yes. International visitors can coordinate with Admissions and International Department.

Can I see clinical practice hospitals?

Hospital visits depend on schedule, permissions and university policy. The clinical practice network is explained on the Clinical Practice page.
