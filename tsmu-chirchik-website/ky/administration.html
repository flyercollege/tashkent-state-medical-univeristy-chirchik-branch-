FAQ

Administration questions.

How do I contact the Director’s Reception?

Use the Director page or contact the Administration Office for appointment routing.

Which office handles student records?

The Registrar Office handles records, transcripts, certificates and academic status.

Who supports international students?

The International Department handles foreign student support, visas, arrival and partnerships.

Where do I ask about tuition and contracts?

Finance Department and Admissions Office provide official financial and contract guidance.
